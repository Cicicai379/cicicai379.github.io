Should I join?

Age

Students aged 11 to 18 are welcome to join this event. There are two levels, beginners and intermediate, and you get to choose which group to join.

Entry Fee

There is no entry fee for the event. Food will be covered by Chinese International School.

Team

You will work in teams of 3-4. If you don't have a team, we will find a team for you!
