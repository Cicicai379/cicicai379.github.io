frequently asked questions

I'm not an experienced programmer. Can I still join?

Absolutely! We'll be running workshops on programming throughout the week for all experience levels, and we'll have mentors available to help. Plus, a team needs more than just coders! Designers, environmentalists, and others are equally important.

Do I have to be in Hong Kong to join?

No. If you can't physically attend the event, you will be able to do a video submission.

Do I have to have a supervisor accompanying me to the event?

No, a supervisor is not required to attend the event.

How does the hackathon work?

The goal of a hackathon is to collaborate with a team to create a project in a short period of time. You'll join as a team and work together during the week to make something cool. Partner companies will present a problem statement they face and your team will create a solution.

What is this year's theme?

The theme for this year is QUALITY EDUCATION. We will post the details soon.
